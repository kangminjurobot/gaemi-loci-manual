The Overview tab gives you an at-a-glance view of the status of every Job.

<Image src={overviewJobsMap} alt="Overview tab full view — list of Job cards grouped by Phase at the top, and Job location markers on a world map at the bottom" />

## Screen Layout

- **Top — Job list**: All Jobs are grouped by the Phase they are currently in.
  - The list is split into three columns: **Phase 1 (PRE-INSTALLATION)**, **Phase 2 (ON-SITE INSTALLATION)**, and **Phase 3 (AREA EXPANSION)**.
  - Each Job card shows the name of the Step currently in progress so you can check work status at a glance.
- **Bottom — Map view**: The service area location of each Job is shown as a marker on a world map.
  - You can also click a marker on the map to select the corresponding Job.

## Selecting a Job

<Image src={overviewJobSelected} alt="Selecting a Job on the Overview tab — the map zooms into the relevant area and a button to move to the Workspace is displayed" />

When you select a Job by clicking its card or map marker:

- The map automatically zooms into the corresponding service area.
- A button for navigating to the **Workspace** tab of the selected Job appears.

:::tip
A typical flow is to review the overall status on the Overview tab, pick the Job you want to work on, and then move to the Workspace tab.
:::
